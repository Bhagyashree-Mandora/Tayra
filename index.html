Tayra - An Incremental Backup Tool for MongoDB

Free your devops from worrying about data corruption or loss. Tayra allows businesses to utilise the power and flexibility of MongoDB whilst protecting their data.

After talking with a number of our customer operations teams, we found a strong case for developing an incremental backup tool for when data backup becomes more and more of a problem as the data size grows. Tayra has been built to resolve this issue and we are already getting positive feedback from our clients.
